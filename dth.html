About DANI TECH HUB

Mission • Vision • Story

Our Mission

To reduce cybercrime by producing world‑class ethical hackers and capable engineers through mentorship, practice, and community support.

Our Focus

Cybersecurity & Ethical Hacking, Mobile App Dev, Web Dev, and Python—taught with hands‑on projects and real‑world problems.

Founder’s Note

“We rise by building together.” — Daniel Ayembe. Inspired by turning setbacks into skills that help people stay safe online.
